About Bluewaves

Nestled along the riverfront with spectacular views, Bluewaves offers an unparalleled dining experience combining contemporary cuisine with traditional techniques. Our elegant dining room provides the perfect backdrop for romantic dinners, business meetings, or special celebrations.

Our executive chef brings over 20 years of culinary expertise, crafting seasonal menus that showcase locally-sourced ingredients and artisanal products. Each dish is meticulously prepared and beautifully presented to delight both the palate and the eye.

Open Tuesday through Sunday from 5 PM to 10 PM, we invite you to experience the art of fine dining at Bluewaves.
